Description
You are a zombie that spawned in a zombie village. The plants have taken over the world, and have now started the plant uprising. 
Your job is to collect resources, rebuild your village, and reclaim civilization.

Features (sorted by priority)
- free roam exploration/movement
- save game
- survival
- inventory/combat (boss fights)
- dungeon crawling/mining (randomly generated)
- multiplayer (pvp/pve coop)
- village building/management
- farming
- potion brewing
